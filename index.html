Why StoxxThema

Being able to design a portfolio based on a theme effectively renders the need for advisors obsolete. The users are able to construct portfolios that reflect their views in a cost efficient manner. Vendors are able to increase the yield on less active tail clients and benefit from incremental productivity of their brokerage platform. StoxxThema allows traditional securities firms to firmly enter the fintech arena.

GENERATING IDEAS

Our program is a concept-driven trading application which allows the user to instantly construct a portfolio based on the user’s view. StoxxThema enables investors to formulate and act upon trading views and insights, unique to the user, on particular themes, such as “water scarcity”, “dogs of the dow” or even “end of fossil fuels industry”. StoxxThema is offered as a white-labeled solution to traditional brokerage and securities houses; it is also provided as a stand-alone solution in specific markets.
