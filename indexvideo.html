WHO WE ARE

Sand Ventures is a Singapore based company and our mission is to create and nurture a fintech ecosystem for the next generation of Asian companies. Because we believe in evolution, rather than revolution, we focus on infrastructure entreprise solutions and we deploy proven business models that are based on sustainable and scalable revenues streams with growth potential. Our team expertise spans across the fintech spectrum, ranging from the most traditional fields of finance to the most advanced and cutting edge software infrastructure technologies.

Our Services

Our business solutions have been adopted by a wide spectrum of institutional clients including private equity firms, hedge funds, financial advisory firms, corporate finance boutiques, family offices, government related firms, and family offices.

Infrastructure solutions

Sand Ventures can readily deploy private market infrastructure solutions for financial intermediaries. We also design the architecture around decentralised autonomous organisations and we provide data security solutions. Sand Ventures’ expertise and know how is deployed across our portfolio of business, and once proven it is put to the service of our clients.

Capital Markets Solutions

We help financial intermediaries address the challenges of cost, control, capital and compliance. We have a unique combination of experience both as managers in large financial institutions, as successful entrepreneurs, which makes us ideal partners. We understand the challenges faced by modern financial intermediaries, as well as their their processes and buying cycles.

Online Lending Platform

Through our native platform, the Total Funding Club, we connects small businesses seeking receivables financing and peer investors in South East Asia. The Total Funding Club acts as a facilitator and operates an online P2B platform, where new lending opportunities are originated and matched thanks to a peer investor network.
